Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

❓ How do I register for a program?

You can register for any program by visiting the Programs page, selecting your desired program, and clicking the "Register Now" or "Enroll Now" button. You'll need to create an account if you haven't already.

❓ What is the difference between free and paid programs?

Free programs are introductory courses that give you a taste of our healing methods. Paid programs are comprehensive, in-depth courses with personalized guidance and certification.

❓ How do I book a consultation?

Visit our Consultation page, choose the type of consultation you need, select your preferred date and time, and complete the booking process.
